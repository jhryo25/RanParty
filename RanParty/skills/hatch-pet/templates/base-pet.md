Requirements:
- Single full-body character, front-facing, neutral pose.
- Arms at sides, standing straight, direct eye contact with viewer.
- This image defines the definitive color palette, proportions, and style for the entire pet.
- Frame size: 192x208 pixels. Single frame (not a strip).
- Output: 192x208 PNG with transparent background.
