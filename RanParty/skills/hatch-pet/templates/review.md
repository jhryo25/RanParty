Generate the REVIEW animation row. Character reviews/completes a task.

Requirements:
- 6-frame horizontal strip, total 1152x208 pixels.
- Frame 1-2: character examines result (head tilts, eyes scan).
- Frame 3-4: processing (thoughtful pose, hand to chin or ear flick).
- Frame 5-6: satisfied nod, small smile, returns to neutral.
- Conveys "checking the work" — thorough but positive.
- 6-frame loop, plays for ~2.6s after turn completion then returns to idle.
